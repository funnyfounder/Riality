How It All Started

During a random fun conversation about converting Iranian paper currency into profit by weight, the RIALITY idea was born. We calculated that with bulk low-value bills, you could technically earn more selling it as paper. Thus, Trash to Cash!

How It Works

  1. 📦 You send us hyperinflated bills
  2. 🌀 We pulp and process them
  3. 💼 We sell the recycled paper at $1.20/kg
  4. 😂 You profit from memes turned materials

Future Vision

RIALITY aims to become the world’s first humorous startup to turn satire into sustainable profit.
